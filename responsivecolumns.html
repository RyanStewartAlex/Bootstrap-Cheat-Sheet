Rows and Columns w/ Responsive Design

Columns resize with screen size. Uses col-xs-*, col-sm-*, col-md-*, col-lg-* to set sizes, and collapse or collapse in for sliding animation.

Collapsable Divs:

Collapse Column 1!

Sit quis officia est consequat adipisicing qui mollit exercitation ea sint consectetur veniam cillum occaecat.

Show Column 2!

Sit quis officia est consequat adipisicing qui mollit exercitation ea sint consectetur veniam cillum occaecat. 

        <div class="row">
            <div class="col-lg-2 col-md-2 col-sm-4 col-xs-12">

                <h2><a href="#col1" data-toggle="collapse">Collapse Column 1!</a></h2>

                <div id="col1" class="collapse in"> <!-- The "in" in Collapse In makes the text show first, then on click close -->
                        Sit quis officia est consequat adipisicing qui mollit exercitation ea sint consectetur veniam cillum occaecat.
                </div>

            </div>

            <div class="col-lg-2 col-md-2 col-sm-4 col-xs-12">

                <h2><a href="#col2" data-toggle="collapse">Show Column 2!</a></h2>

                <div id="col2" class="collapse"> <!-- The lack of "in" in Collapse makes the text show later on click -->

        </div>
